Goal for site

I would like for this to serve as a personal portfolio moving forward.
Thus I wanted to keep the design simplistic while showing information immediately.
Thats why I added the recent works section, so that someone, for instance a client or recruiter viewing doesnt need to go searching for things off the bat.
